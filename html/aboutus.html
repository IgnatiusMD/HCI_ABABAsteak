ABABA Steak is a famous fine dining restaurant specialized in steak gourmet. ABABA Steak was first discovered in 2021 by a group of students from SUNIB University majoring in Computer Science. These students first served their dish to students majoring in Hotel and Management of SUNIB University and they approved the taste of ABABA Steak’s special ingredients. These students realize that they shouldn’t keep the delicousness of ABABA Steak to themselves and wanted to share it with the rest of the world!
